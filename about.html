NITDA Academy For Research And Training

The National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) is empowered by Section 6(a) of NITDA Act 2007 to “create a frame work for the planning, research, development, standardization, application, coordination, monitoring, evaluation and regulation of Information Technology practices, activities and systems in Nigeria and all matters related thereto and for that purpose, and which without detracting from the generality of the foregoing shall include providing universal access for Information Technology and systems penetration including rural, urban and under-served areas”. The Agency is further mandated by Section 6(h) to create incentives to promote the use of information technology in all spheres of life in Nigeria including the development of guidelines for setting up of information technology systems and knowledge parks. The NITDA Academy concept is in furtherance of development of Knowledge Parks that would empower Nigerians to utilize Information Technology in a positive and productive manner.

 

Furthermore, Section 21 of NITDA Act 2007 empowers the Agency to ‘apply the proceeds of its Fund established under Section 12 of its enabling Act to any other expenditure in connection with all or any of its functions under this Act.’ Pursuant to its powers, the Agency has organised different capacity building programmes to develop globally competitive human capital in Information Technology (IT) sector with emphasis on creating and supporting a knowledge- based economy.

 

In order to consolidate the unprecedented successes recorded through these capacity building programmes, the Agency conceived the idea of setting-up an Academy that will serve as the Foremost Centre of Excellence for Research and Development (R&D) in Emerging Technologies in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and related entrepreneurial skills. The NITDA Academy for Research and Training (NART), hereafter referred to as NITDA Academy is envisioned to strengthen National technological capabilities of Nigerians in order to bridge the professional skills gap between industry and academia, while also promoting and inclusive ICT driven society.

Objectives

The objectives of the NITDA Academy is to:

a. Build capacity and develop skills of participants in the knowledge and application of emerging IT programs and systems
b. Complement the efforts of tertiary institutions in IT education by focusing on developing marketable skills and knowledge
c. Bridge industry-academia research gap that focuses on addressing prioritized national challenges (security, digital services promotion, innovation and entrepreneurship)
d. To employ a hybrid training using pedagogical and andragogy techniques
e. To ensure effective learning outcomes and instruction employing the Dick and Carey instructional design approach

Scope

The scope of works covers the following:

1. GOVERNANCE, MANAGEMENT AND CONTROL: ACADEMY MANAGEMENT, FRAMEWORK, RESEARCH, MONITORING & EVALUATION
- The development of the management structures and other control and monitoring mechanisms for the academy including standard operating procedures
- Facilitation of Partnership Collaborations with Industry for Internships and onboarding
- To conduct Training Needs assessment, Gap Analysis and Statistics
- To conduct Certification of instructors - Pilot Phase
- To Develop Impact Assessment methodology of Academy
- To Develop Monitoring and Evaluation plan including Indicators and MOVs
- To Provide Certification of Trainees
- To Provide Media Publication and Advertisement Jingles & Fliers
- To facilitate the Mentorship Program and Champion’s development initiative
